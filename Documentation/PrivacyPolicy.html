Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how this app (“the App”) handles your information.

Core Principles

  • On‑device only: All data you create or open in the App is stored only on your device.
  • No network access: The App does not connect to the internet. Nothing ever leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it.
  • No collection, no tracking: We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. By design, we cannot access your content.

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not collect analytics, usage metrics, crash reports, or identifiers.
  • We do not use third‑party SDKs or trackers.
  • We do not store data on external servers or cloud services.

Your Choices

Any time you choose to export, copy, or share content using your operating system’s share or file features, that action is under your control. The App does not automatically transmit any data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above and include the revised policy in the app repository or website.
